# jrnl dashboard

A local, graphical dashboard for your [jrnl](https://github.com/jrnl-org/jrnl) journal data.

Open `dashboard.html` in your browser, drop your jrnl file, and explore your entries with heatmaps, tag filters, and full-text search.

## Features

- **Calendar heatmap** — see your journaling activity at a glance (Mon–Sun weeks)
- **Timeline view** — scroll through entries grouped by month
- **Tag filtering** — click tags to filter, see counts, combine multiple tags
- **Full-text search** — search across all entries in real-time
- **Click-to-navigate** — click a day on the heatmap to jump to that day's entries

## Usage

1. Open `dashboard.html` in any modern browser
2. Drag your jrnl `.txt` file onto the page (or click to browse)
3. Everything runs locally — no data ever leaves your machine
